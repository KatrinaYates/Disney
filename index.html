Making a World of Difference for 50 Years


At Walt Disney World Resort we are honored to be part of the Central Florida community, a place we have called home since Oct 1, 1971.


Fifty years have brought many changes to our community and to our company but one thing that hasn’t changed is our desire to create a positive impact. We believe our community is at its best when we collaborate as volunteers, neighbors and friends.

We hope to continue to serve as a beacon of hope and happiness, bringing the magic of Disney to our guests and children and families across our theme parks, resorts, and community.

We invite you to explore how we continue investing in our communities and create a brighter future for all who live, work and play in our region.
